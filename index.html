The building has two entrances on King Street West. Look for Reedham House signage. We're located in the main building - if you're unsure which entrance, call us on arrival.

Getting Here

Easy access from Manchester city centre

🚊 From Piccadilly Tram Station

  • Exit Piccadilly tram station onto Piccadilly Gardens
  • Walk west along Market Street towards Deansgate
  • Turn right onto King Street
  • Continue as King Street becomes King Street West
  • Reedham House is on your right, just past the junction with Quay Street
⏱️ 12-15 min walk

🚗 By Car

  • From A57(M) take exit for Deansgate/City Centre
  • Follow signs for A56 Chester Road
  • Turn right onto King Street West
  • Reedham House is approximately 100m on your left
  • Free parking available on-site - please let us know ahead of time to reserve
🅿️ Free parking on-site
